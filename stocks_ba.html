The Boeing Company (BA) in the Stock Market

We first begin our investigation with Boeing (BA). By far, it is the largest of the five in our list in terms of market capitalization, defined as the market price of its stock multiplied by the total number of shares. Given its relatively large position within the stock market, Boeing continues to draw investors over its latest developments in commercial, military, and space aircraft. From a national security standpoint, the latter has especially become more significant upon the establishment of the US Space Force in 2019. You will have the opportunity to evaluate its historical closing prices and annual returns from 1985 to 2021, as well as its most significant absolute and relative changes in its stock over that time. The ten trading sessions exhibiting its highest volumes, along with its frequency shares with respect to certain closing price and volume ranges, are also included.
